S T Y L E - A I D style

No matter what. Feeling comfortable in your skin is the top priority in achieving your version of pristine style. Those thin lace dresses worn by professional super models may or may not be your cup of tea. It is your job (and ours) to make sure you find your main go-to style when it comes to your personal tastes and needs. These references below will aid in most, if not, all of your fashion requirements.

C O L O R - B Y - C O L O R

Picking colors isn't always simple. But knowing the colors that make your melanin pop as well as reflect your personality makes confidence achievable!

Depending on your complexion, certain colors will compliment your skin tone more than others. For darker skin tones and richer pigmentation, opt for pops of color that will really make both you and your outfit spring to life! These include bright reds and yellows. For fairer skin tones, darker shades of color will really highlight your stylish apparel. Essentially, try to pick colors that you have noticed in the past to flatter yourself! It's always okay to experiment with colors and patterns as well so feel free to do so!

P E R S O N A L - P E R S O N A

Getting a feel for what your go-to style may be is never an easy task. But by collecting your characteristics and understanding your body type, it'll be much easier to understand what your fashion niches are.

If you cannot stand heels or sneakers then that's okay! Try flats or open-toed shoes. Knowing yourself enough to not wear heels because you cannot stand them

S E A S O N A L - I N S P I R A T I O N

From season-to-season, we learn that styles come and go as time progresses. But thanks to time, we have new motivation to shop for all the hottest trends! Knowing what is hot and what is not is not so simple - and even knowing the trends of each season may be even more difficult if you're not so 'seasoned' in fashion. Dressing yourself according to the the time of year will reflect your wisdom of fashion as well as how much you value your appearance

Spring x Summer

The warm seasons call for all of the fresh styles summer and spring have to offer. After assessing what colors make you look great as well as understanding what you can and cannot pull off, it is time to throw the sweaters back in the closet! Spring and Summer are the times for fun maxi dresses, loose blouses, and athletic wear. Schools out! And the warm seasons inspire fun times with friends and family. Opt for playful and lively apparel if half of it isn't athletic gear anyway!

Fall x Winter

The chilly Fall and Winter eves are the prime seasons of fashion showcasing. The cold seasons call for more articles of clothing and, in other words, more opportunities to express yourself! Boots (all boots), cardigans and sweaters are all staples of cool and crisp weather. Keeping yourself warm can look great if you are conscious of what is in season.
