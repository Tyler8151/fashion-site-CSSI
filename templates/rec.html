S T Y L E - A I D style

No matter what. Feeling comfortable in your skin is the top priority in achieving your pristine style. Those thin lace dresses worn by professional super models may or may not be your cup of tea. It is your job (and ours) to make sure you find your main go-to style when it comes to your personal tastes and needs. These references below will help aid in most, if not, all of your fashion requirements.

C O L O R - B Y - C O L O R

Picking colors isn't always simple. But knowing the colors that make your melanin pop as well as reflect your personality makes confidence achievable!

Depending on your complexion, certain colors will compliment your skin tone more than others. For darker skin tones and richer pigmentation, opt for pops of color that will really make both you and your outfit spring to life! These include bright reds and yellows. For fairer skin tones, darker shades of color will really highlight your stylish apparel. Essentially, try to pick colors that you have noticed in the past to flatter yourself! Always feel free to experiment with colors and patterns!

P E R S O N A L - P E R S O N A

Getting a feel for what your go-to style may be is never an easy task. But by collecting your characteristics and understanding your body type, it'll be much easier to understand what your fashion niches are.
